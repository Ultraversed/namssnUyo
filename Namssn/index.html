Frequently asked questions

We are often asked...

How do I pay my departmental dues?

Due to an existing issue with the student's association bank account, Visit the NAMSSN secretariat and you will be told what to do!

What are the different career paths available to math majors?

Math majors can pursue knowledge-based paths, such as academia, theoretical physics, or cryptology. Alternatively, they can follow skills-based paths that apply mathematical tools to industry challenges, including data science, actuarial science, quantitative finance, and operations research, where the emphasis is on leveraging software and statistical techniques to solve practical problems.

How can I run for a position in NAMSSN?

To run for a position in NAMSSN, you need to meet the eligibility criteria during the designated election period. More details will be communicated by the director of information or by contacting the NAMSSN secretariat.

How can I submit a complaint or suggestion regarding the current curriculum or department policies?

You can submit a complaint or suggestion regarding the current curriculum or department policies by contacting the NAMSSN secretariat or visiting the student services office.

What are the course requirements to gain admission into Mathematics?

O'Level Requirements (WAEC, NECO, GCE). You must have at least five (5) credit level passes in your Senior Secondary School Certificate or its equivalent in not more than two sittings. The compulsory subjects usually include: English Language, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and One other Science subject (such as Biology, Further Mathematics, Agricultural Science, or Economics). for further information on O'Level requirements, please visit the JAMB website or the university's admission page for the most accurate and up-to-date information.

Where can I find past exam papers or study guides?

Everyone has access to our digital resource library through our website, which includes curated study notes and practice problems for major departmental exams.

What is the full meaning of NAMSSN?

NAMSSN stands for the National Association of Mathematical science Students of Nigeria.
